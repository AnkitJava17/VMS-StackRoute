Carpool help centre

How do I book a carpool ride?

You can book a carpool ride on blablacar.com. Simply search for your destination, choose the date you want to travel and pick the carpool that suits you best!

How much does a carpool ride cost?

The costs of a carpool ride can vary greatly, and depend on factors like distance, time of departure, the demand of that ride and more.

How do I cancel my carpool ride?

If you have a change of plans, you can always cancel your carpool ride from the ‘My Bookings’ section of our website. The sooner you cancel, the better. That way the driver has time to accept new passengers.
